Commitment to Privacy

At MetroLabX, we respect and value your privacy. We are committed to protecting all personal information shared with us and handling it responsibly, transparently, and in line with global privacy standards.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to deliver our services, such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, and project-related details submitted through our forms or direct communication.

Use of Your Information

Any information you provide is used strictly for communication, service delivery, support, and internal operational purposes within MetroLabX.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information, including email addresses and contact details, with any third party outside MetroLabX.

Data Security

We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting MetroLabX directly.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be published on this page.
